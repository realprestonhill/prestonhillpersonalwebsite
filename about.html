About

Who is Preston Hill?

“I am very proud to be a Tar Heel.”

Fox News

I’m Preston Hill, a sophomore at UNC-Chapel Hill double majoring in Political Science and Journalism on the pre-law track. I’m passionate about law, media, and public service, and I’ve built a strong foundation of experience through writing, leadership, and political engagement.

Currently, I serve as President of the UNC-Chapel Hill College Republicans, President of the Carolina Liberty Foundation, Secretary of the North Carolina Federation of College Republicans, and President of Turning Point USA at UNC. My work and advocacy have been featured in The Daily Tar Heel, Reuters, The Wall Street Journal, Fox News, Politico, and The Boston Globe.

In addition to my leadership roles, I’m a case brief and journal writer for the Undergraduate Law Review at Carolina and a contributor to the Carolina Review journal. I’ve worked and interned on several high-profile campaigns during the 2024 election cycle, including Donald J. Trump for President, State Senator Michael Lee, and Dan Bishop for Attorney General. I’ve also organized and hosted numerous political candidates and speakers on campus.

Outside of school and politics, I enjoy running, reading, reviewing films, and connecting with new people. I’m always looking for opportunities to grow, collaborate, and make an impact in the fields of law, media, and public policy.

Leadership

Organizations and positions.

UNC-Chapel Hill College Republicans

President

Since joining College Republicans my freshman year, I have served as Vice President and most recently President. Over the past two years, I have worked with numerous North Carolina candidates during the 2024 election season and campaigned for Republicans both locally and nationally.

Turning Point USA

President

Since joining the TPUSA chapter at Chapel Hill my freshman year, I was elected Treasurer in 2024 and President in 2025. During my tenure, I have helped host speakers including Ben Carson, Andrew Clavan, Riley Gaines, Candace Owens, Charlie Kirk, and Vivek Ramaswamy.

North Carolina Federation of College Republicans

Secretary

As Secretary of NCFCR, I work with the President to maintain records, coordinate with other College Republican chapters, grow membership, and support statewide events.

Carolina Liberty Foundation

President

As President of Carolina Liberty Foundation, I oversee the Carolina Review and help coordinate student engagement events, including speaking events like Wisconsin Governor Scott Walker.

Undergraduate Law Society at Carolina

Brief Writer

At ULAC, I serve on the Moot Court team as a Brief Writer, competing in national competitions against other universities across the country to answer unresolved legal questions.
